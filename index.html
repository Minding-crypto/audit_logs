What are News Sources?
These are the RSS feeds (news websites) the bot checks for articles. You can add new ones, remove old ones, or temporarily disable a source without deleting it.

When adding a source, you choose what type of website it is this tells the bot how strictly to filter it:
  • Auto + cyber website — already focused on automotive cybersecurity. Every article gets sent (no keyword filtering).
  • General cybersecurity website — broad security news. Bot applies keyword filtering to find automotive-related articles.
  • singapore news website — local news. Bot looks for singapore-relevant cybersecurity stories.
Connect to Supabase on the Settings tab first to manage sources.

How do keywords work?
When the bot finds a article frm a general cybersecurity website, it checks for keywords to decide if the article is relevant. There are different keyword lists for different purposes, select a list on the left to view and edit it.To add a keyword, just type it and press Enter or click Add.

Controls how aggressively the bot filters general cybersecurity articles.
Strict (recommended): Article must contain an automotive word AND a cybersecurity action word (e.g. "vulnerability", "exploit", "hack", "breach", "CVE"). Fewer false positives — less noise in Telegram.
